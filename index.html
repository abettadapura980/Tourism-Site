Things to Do

Beaches

One of the things that you can do is visit one of the many beaches that are located in Maui. Some of the most popular beaches include Kaanapali Beach, Oneloa Beach, and Palauea Beach.

Water activities

There are also many water activies to do in Maui. Some water activities are snorkeling, whale watching, and jetskiing.

Land Activities

One land activity that you can do in Maui is driving on the Road to Hana. Other land activities include going ziplining and watching a Luau.
